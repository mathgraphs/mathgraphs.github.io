About Me

Academic Background
Bachelor degree in Mathematics and Physics, and a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science (Graph Theory, Combinatorial Theory, Topological Design of Computer Networks).
Speak fluently English, Spanish, Russian, and Italian.

Teaching/Tutoring Experience
More than 15 years of experience teaching elementary, high school, and university courses of Mathematics. Among them, Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Calculus, and Advanced Courses of Mathematics at University Level.

I worked for Private Schools and different learning centres in Richmond Hill, Brampton, and Toronto. My students were able to improve their grades in a very short amount of time and several of them learned to enjoy working on Mathematics not loving the subject very much at the beginning.

I also had the chance to teach English to students ESL. I know how the process of learning a new language happens. English is not my first language, but I speak very clearly and I have a good understanding of its grammar structure. You will improve your writing and speaking skills in a very short amount of time.

I also had students with learning disabilities. I have a very good idea how to deal with different techniques such as Davis Dyslexia Correction Program and Kumon Program.
